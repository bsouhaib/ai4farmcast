Aim for Scale AI Weather Training Program

Weather Forecasting & AI for Agricultural Services

September 22-26, 2025 | MBZUAI Campus & NCM, UAE

Revolutionizing Weather Forecasting for Farmers

Aim for Scale is committed to scaling cost-effective innovations that improve the livelihoods of farmers in low- and middle-income countries. Our training program brings together meteorologists, agricultural specialists, and AI researchers to explore how recent advances in artificial intelligence are creating new opportunities in medium-range to subseasonal forecasts for agricultural applications.

Program Mission

Hands-on tutorials to run and evaluate AI weather models, joint landscape mapping, and recommendations for integrating forecasts into agricultural decision-making to benefit hundreds of millions of farmers.

Technical Track

Learn to run cutting-edge AI models like AIFS, FuXi, and GraphCast. Focus on benchmarking, data quality assessment, and operational implementation considerations for national weather services.

Applied Track

Explore climate services delivery, farmer decision-making contexts, communication pathways, and end-to-end agricultural support systems for effective weather information dissemination.

Joint Collaboration

Collaborative work on operational examples, landscape mapping, and building concepts of operations (CONOPS) for country-specific implementation of AI-enhanced weather services.
